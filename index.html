Team & History

Founded in 1905, Chelsea Football Club has grown into one of the most iconic teams in the world. Here, we celebrate the rich history and legacy that defines the spirit of the club.

Club Timeline

1905

Chelsea Football Club Founded.

1955

First League Title won.

1965

First FA Cup Victory.

1997

FA Cup glory at Wembley Stadium.

2005

A Centenary celebration with a Premier League win.

2012

Historic Champions League win in Munich.

2015

League title secured under manager José Mourinho.

2021

Victory in the Champions League Final in Porto.

Legendary Figures

Frank Lampard

Chelsea's all-time leading scorer, renowned for his midfield prowess and remarkable goal-scoring ability.

John Terry

A symbol of leadership and defense, Terry is one of the most decorated captains in the club's history.

Didier Drogba

Legendary striker known for his physical strength and crucial goals, especially in cup finals and crucial matches.

Gianfranco Zola

One of the most talented and creative forwards, Zola dazzled fans with his skill and sportsmanship.

Memorable Matches

1970 FA Cup Final

A significant victory over Leeds United in a replay match at Old Trafford, marking Chelsea's first FA Cup win.

2005 Premier League Win

Under José Mourinho, Chelsea secures the Premier League title with a record 95 points, setting a high standard for future successes.

2012 Champions League Final

Chelsea's historic win against Bayern Munich in their home ground, securing their first Champions League title.

2013 Europa League Final

Chelsea clinches the Europa League title against Benfica, becoming the first English club to win all three major UEFA club trophies.
